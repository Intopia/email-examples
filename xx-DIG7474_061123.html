Want saving to be effortless?

Here's how to set up a recurring transfer¹ from another CommBank account.

In the CommBank app:
  1. Once you log in, go to 'Pay' then select 'Transfer between accounts'.
  2. Enter your transfer details and select 'Transfer' on the top right.
  3. Select 'Create a recurring transfer' and follow the prompts.

In NetBank:
  1. Once you log in, go to 'Transfers & BPAY®'.
  2. Select your accounts to transfer 'From' and 'To'.
  3. Under 'When', select 'Set up regular payments' and follow the prompts.
