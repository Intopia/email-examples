Say goodbye to your monthly account fee. Here's how.

%%=v(@Salutation)=%%

To help you get the most out of your Smart Access account, we'd like to remind you that you might be able to avoid the monthly account fee we normally charge on the first day of each month.

Here's some ways you can avoid the monthly account fee:
 
Meet the minimum deposit
Deposit at least $2,000 into your Smart Access account each calendar month (excludes Bank initiated transactions) and we'll waive the monthly account fee on that account.

Here are two ways that could help you do this:

• Get paid straight into your account
Share your Smart Access account details (BSB & Account Number) with your employer. You can find them on NetBank or in the CommBank app.
   
• Set up a regular transfer from another account
You can do this by visiting NetBank > Transfers & BPAY > Scheduled transfers.
 
 
Talk to us about other monthly account fee waivers
You may be eligible to have your monthly account fee waived if an Australian Age, Service or Disability Pension is directly credited to your account, you have a balance of $50,000 or more in eligible contributing accounts, are reliant on over the counter services because of a disability or have an eligible home loan.
 
To find out if you meet the eligibility criteria for one of these options or to talk to us, please visit any CommBank branch or message us in the CommBank app.
